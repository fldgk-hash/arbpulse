Most arbitrage tools are either paywalled, delayed by 30+ seconds, or built on simulated data. **ArbPulse Pro was built differently** вҖ” it connects directly to WebSocket feeds from 4 major exchanges and 5+ Solana DEXs simultaneously, computes net spreads after real fees and slippage, and surfaces only opportunities that actually make sense to execute.

ArbPulse Pro continuously scans two arenas:

### рҹҡҖ Solana DEX Arbitrage
Monitors cross-DEX price discrepancies for trending Solana tokens across:

| DEX | Fee |
|-----|-----|
| Raydium | 0.25% |

| Jupiter | 0.20% |
| Pump.fun | 1.00% |

For every token trading on 2+ DEXs, it calculates the real net profit after fees + slippage and flags anything above your threshold.

### вҡЎ CEX Arbitrage
Live WebSocket feeds from all four exchanges:

- **Binance** вҖ” book ticker stream
- **OKX** вҖ” tickers v5
- **Bybit** вҖ” spot stream
- **Kraken** вҖ” ticker subscription

Computes both **triangular arbitrage** (e.g. BTCвҶ’ETHвҶ’SOLвҶ’USDT on Binance) and **cross-exchange arbitrage** (buy on Bybit, sell on OKX).
